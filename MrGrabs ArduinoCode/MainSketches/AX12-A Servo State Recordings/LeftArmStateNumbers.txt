States represent positions of the arm, made up of 7 AX12-A servos. 
At these positions each servo will be at its own unique position which is stored in memory on the servo. 
One can move the arm around into desirable positions and then read the position value of all servos at their specific memory addresses.
For more information on how to do this, refer to MrGrabs Construction Guide, 'AX12-A Smart Robotic Arm'
